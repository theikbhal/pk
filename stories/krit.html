How I Started A $91K/Month Software Agency After A Failed Product Launch

December 2nd, 2019 $91K MRR

About the Founder

Andrew Askins is the founder of Krit, a software agency specializing in helping non-technical founders build their Minimum Lovable Products. With a background in both design and development, Andrew and his team focus on creating beautiful, well-coded applications that help their clients bring their ideas to life.

The Business

Krit is a software agency that partners with non-technical founders to design and build their applications from the ground up. They work with clients who have prototyped their ideas using spreadsheets or no-code tools but need professional software development to bring their vision to life. The agency has helped various clients achieve significant success, including helping an attorney grow her legal tech startup to $1M in Annual Run Rate and assisting a 71-year-old financial consultant in building his first tech company with prestigious clients like Yale, Brown, NYU, and Dartmouth.
