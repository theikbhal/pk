How I Created A $2K/Month Startup Marketing Database Using No-Code Tools

May 19th, 2021 $2K MRR

About the Founder

Xavier Coiffard is an IndieHacker and solo entrepreneur from Le Lamentin, Martinique. With 12 years of experience as a developer, including freelancing and working with startups, he embarked on a challenge to build and ship 6 products in 6 months. Spreadtheworld was one of his successful ventures, alongside other products like IdeaHunt, RemoteFR, and UserBooster.

The Business

Spreadtheworld is a comprehensive database of 400+ places where startups can post their products and services for marketing purposes. The platform helps startups find the right channels to promote their products, making it easier to reach their target audience and gain visibility in the startup ecosystem.
