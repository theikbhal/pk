How I Launched A $200K/Month Gmail Mail Merge Tool

March 5th, 2020 GMass, Inc.

About the Founder

Ajay Goel, founder of GMass, Inc., created a platform for sending cold emails and email marketing campaigns with Gmail. The tool works as a Chrome extension and has gained over 500,000 user signups since its launch in 2015.

Key Metrics

  • Monthly Revenue: $450K
  • User Base: 500,000+ signups
  • Team Size: 1 Founder, 1 Employee
  • Location: Dayton, Ohio, USA

Notable Customers

GMass serves a wide range of customers, from entrepreneurs to major tech companies including:

  • Uber
  • Lyft
  • LinkedIn
  • Twitter
  • Indeed
  • Google
  • Salesforce.com
  • Snapchat

Backstory

Ajay has extensive experience in email marketing, having previously founded JangoMail in 2002, which he ran for 10 years before its acquisition in 2012.

Key Learnings

  • Focus on solving a specific problem (email marketing in Gmail)
  • Build for both small businesses and enterprise customers
  • Leverage existing platforms (Gmail) to reach a wider audience
  • Maintain simplicity while offering powerful features
