How I Launched A Profitable Earrings Business In One Year

March 28th, 2019 $260K MRR

About the Founder

Jackie Burke is the founder of Tini Lux, a fashion earrings business based in New York. She started her business in November 2017 while working full-time as an engineer, planning her wedding, and moving from Washington, DC to New Jersey. Despite the initial challenges of balancing multiple responsibilities, she successfully grew Tini Lux into a profitable business within a year.

The Business

Tini Lux is the first line of fashion earrings made specifically for people with metal allergies and sensitive ears. The business targets women between the ages of 18 and 45 who were previously unable to wear earrings due to sensitive skin or allergies. The brand has developed a loyal following, with many customers expressing emotional gratitude for being able to wear earrings again after years of being unable to do so.
