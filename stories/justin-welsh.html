How Justin Welsh Built A $2M Solopreneur Empire

April 12th, 2023 $317K MRR

About the Founder

Justin Welsh is a writer and solopreneur from Accord, NY, USA, who has built a portfolio of one-person internet businesses through social media. He has achieved remarkable success with a lifestyle-first approach, running his business with high automation and only a part-time VA. His social media presence is impressive, with 380K followers on LinkedIn, 330K followers on Twitter, and 80K weekly readers of his newsletter, "The Saturday Solopreneur."

The Business

Justin's core business is a knowledge business focused on creating digital products that help other solopreneurs and entrepreneurs leverage social media to build their one-person businesses online. His flagship products include digital courses like "The LinkedIn Operating System," which teaches people how to effectively use social media for business growth.
