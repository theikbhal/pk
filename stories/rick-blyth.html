I Developed A Niche Chrome Extension For Amazon Merch Sellers That Makes $120K/Year

April 19th, 2022 Manchester, UK

About the Founder

Rick Blyth is a 20-year career developer who transitioned from a corporate tech director role to building successful Micro SaaS products. His journey began after reading "Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki, which inspired him to seek passive income opportunities.

The Business

Rick developed two main products:

  • Merch Wizard: A Chrome extension for Amazon Merch sellers to organize and manage their listings & designs
  • KDP Wizard: A similar tool for Amazon's Kindle Direct Publishing platform

Key metrics:

  • $10K Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
  • 1 Founder
  • 0 Employees

Key Learnings

"Embrace failure. Move quickly, fail fast, and fail forward."
  • Work on your business, not in your business
  • Build to sell from day one
  • Don't assume you know what your target audience wants
  • Start marketing while building (20% of time)

Tech Stack

  • Gumroad for licensing
  • Airtable for data management
  • WordPress & WooCommerce
  • Zapier for automation
  • MailChimp for email marketing

Current Status

Rick successfully sold his Micro SaaS business and is now focused on helping other developers build their own profitable bootstrapped apps through his free Micro SaaS Handbook and YouTube channel.
