How Davis Baer Used Unorthodox Marketing To Reach $1,000 MRR

January 14th, 2019 Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA

About the Founder

Davis Baer is the co-founder of OneUp, a social media scheduling tool that helps businesses automate their social media posts. With a background in finance and experience in startup marketing, Davis transitioned from corporate finance to building a successful SaaS product.

The Business

OneUp is a social media scheduling tool that differentiates itself by offering:

  • Automatic post repetition at custom intervals
  • Support for multiple platforms (Google My Business, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Twitter thread scheduling capabilities

Key metrics:

  • $100K Monthly Revenue
  • 2 Founders
  • 4 Employees

Key Marketing Strategy

"I wanted to take advantage of us being small and, as Paul Graham has famously said, 'do things that don't scale' by really wow-ing the users during the onboarding experience."

Their unique approach included:

  • Personal video messages using Loom for new signups
  • 10x content strategy for SEO
  • Product Hunt launches for visibility
  • Customer feedback-driven feature development

Tech Stack

  • Loom for personalized videos
  • Crisp for customer support
  • Slack for internal communication
  • Trello for product roadmap
  • Mixpanel, Google Analytics, and Hotjar for analytics

Key Learnings

  • Personalization can be a powerful differentiator
  • Customer feedback should drive product development
  • Consistency is key to success
  • Focus on creating 10x better content than competitors
